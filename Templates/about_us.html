The Mission

Financial literacy is often the "missing course" in a student's curriculum. YourTreasurer was born from the need to bridge the gap between receiving a monthly allowance and making it last until the final day.

Designed specifically for the campus ecosystem, we don't just track numbers; we manage relationships and liabilities. From splitting canteen bills with friends to ensuring your hostel rent is paid on time, YourTreasurer acts as your personal digital CFO.

Core Pillars

  • Proactive Alerts: We notify you before you hit your limit, not after.
  • Social Accountability: Automated reminders for friends who "forgot" to pay you back.
  • Analytical Insight: Visualizing where your money goes—from junk food to education.
  • Privacy First: Securely managing your financial proof with Cloudinary and MongoDB.
