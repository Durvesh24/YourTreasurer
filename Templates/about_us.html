The Mission

Financial literacy is often the "missing course" in a student's curriculum. YourTreasurer was born from the need to bridge the gap between receiving a monthly allowance and making it last until the final day.

Designed specifically for the campus ecosystem, we don't just track numbers; we manage relationships and liabilities. From splitting canteen bills with friends to ensuring your hostel rent is paid on time, YourTreasurer acts as your personal digital CFO.

Core Pillars

⚡ Proactive Alerts

We actively notify you before you hit your budget limits using our advanced warning systems, not just after you're already in debt.

🤝 Social Accountability

Stop losing money to bad memory. Automated debt reminders ensure friends who "forgot" to pay you back get a polite email nudge.

📊 Analytical Insight

Stop guessing where your money vanishes. We intelligently categorize and visualize your spending so you know exactly what is bleeding your wallet dry.

🔒 Privacy First Architecture

Securely managing your financial receipts directly with Cloudinary, and encrypting your data at rest on robust MongoDB Atlas clusters.
